Lore

Neo Simualator 2024 is a game that takes place in a school inspired by a swedish school.

In this game you are a wheelchair bound student named Neo with a quest to be able to walk.

In order to meet this goal you are required to hustle and grid to eventually afford robot legs

But in order to accuire those robotic legs funds you need to turn to the less legal of methods

This include selling potentoally stolen and or ilegal contraband to your fellow classmates

But be careful! the teachers are sharp and will chase you down if the catch you breaking the rules

If you are caught possessing too much, selling, or buying contraband, they will take your hard earn items and increase your detention score!

Too much detention score and you will be kicked out of school and your life will be ruined! (Your poor familty will stop reiving social security and get evicted)
